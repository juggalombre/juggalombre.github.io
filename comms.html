COMMISSION RULES:

Commissions are generally open all the time; you can contact me at any of the social media sites at the bottom of the page. Below are the rules for prospective art pieces; what I will/won't draw, how you are allowed to use my work, etc.

Commission payments will be handled via Ko-Fi.

TERMS OF USE AND SERVICE:

While I'm working on your commission...

  • You may ask for changes before I finish the lineart. Once I finish the lineart and send you the appropriate WIP, you cannot ask for further changes. However, you may ask for corrections if I've made a mistake.
  • You may ask for additional WIPs if you wish to see them; I try to send one wip per phase of the piece.

    • Once finished, you MAY:

  • Use my icon as you profile picture on social media, so long as you use the Signed version and Credit me!
  • Repost the Signed version to your social media platforms, so long as you Credit me!

    • You MAY NOT:

  • Mint my art on the blockchain. If found, I will report the listing and warn others of your behavior.
  • Edit my art and pass it off as your own.
